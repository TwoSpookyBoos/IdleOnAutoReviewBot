Introduction

Our website uses cookies and local storage to enhance your experience and provide personalized services. This Cookie Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how you can manage your preferences.

What are Cookies and Local Storage?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. Local storage is a form of web storage that allows websites to store data in the browser on a user's device. While cookies are sent with HTTP requests to the server, local storage data is only accessible on the client side.

Information We Collect

The only personal data we collect and store in local storage is your game username. This information is used to retrieve and display your game progress. Additionally, your username is logged in our access and error logs to help us diagnose and resolve issues, and to monitor the use of our website. No other personal data is collected, and the username is not shared with third parties.

How We Use Your Information

  • Personalization: We use your game username to personalize your experience on our website by retrieving and displaying your game progress.
  • Functionality: Storing the username in local storage helps us maintain the state of your game session and provide a seamless experience.
  • Logging: The username is logged in access and error logs to help us diagnose and resolve technical issues, ensure the security of our services, and monitor the website's usage.
